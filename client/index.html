PERSONAL

I identify as a perfectionist who learned that practicality and punctuality are just as pertinent. That being said, however, I can just as easily call myself a dreamer void of reason. I say this because I recognize that the common delusions of grandeur that roam my thoughts are not so common for others. Consequently, I will not settle for anything less than a legacy to leave behind on this earth. My playtime, my relationships, my work and anything that I do is backed by a love of life and a love for others... in a deep but platonic kind of way. My enemy is not others' dreams but the doubt that sprouts from within me.

Different people live different lives and, well, live for different reasons. I live for experience over living for objects. The reason being that knowledge is addicting, not to say that accumulating things isn't, but my knowledge informs me that objects are less engaging than the knowledge used to interact with objects. Thus, I choose the more rich in entertainment. My interest in experience, however, is just an interest and not a direct correlation to my intelligence. I'm just average with a hint of me, which I suppose is just like most people. I have to say I enjoy the view from atop this bell curve, it's quite holistic.
